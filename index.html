Spacetime is a beatboxer, producer, rapper, and open format DJ rooted in Saint Louis. His sound fuses hip-hop, beatboxing, and genre-blending creativity — seamlessly moving from hip-hop to house without missing a beat.

With a love for all electronic styles and a deep catalog on SoundCloud and streaming platforms, every set is an experience that warps time and space on the dance floor.
